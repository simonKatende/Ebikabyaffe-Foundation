Mmamba

Ekika kya Mmamba
23,481 members · 1,204 Bataka-verified
Royal Court · Kingdom of Buganda

⚓ Gabunga — Admiral of the Royal Navy

The Mmamba clan commands the Kabaka's royal fleet on Lake Victoria and controls the Sese Islands — the most strategic waterway of Buganda.

Omuziro🐟 Lungfish
Akabbiro🦌 Muguya (Kob)
OmutakaNdiwalana
OriginKintu era

Origin Story

The Mmamba trace their origin to Kintu, the first Kabaka of Buganda, through Nambi's lineage. The lungfish — which survives land and water, enduring dry seasons buried in earth — symbolises Mmamba resilience.

Sigas (Sub-clan branches)

Nkima — Monkey siga · Central Buganda
Kasimba — Genet Cat siga · Masaka / Rakai
Nkerebwe — Crocodile siga · Sese Islands / Gabunga fleet

Taboos (Mizizo)

A Mmamba may not eat the lungfish (omuziro)
A Mmamba may not eat Muguya (kob antelope — akabbiro)
A lungfish found on land must be returned to water
A Mmamba may not marry another Mmamba — exogamy observed
