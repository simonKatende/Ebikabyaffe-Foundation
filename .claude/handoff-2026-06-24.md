# Ebikabyaffe Foundation вЂ” Session Handoff В· 24 June 2026

## What this project is
**Ebikabyaffe Foundation Member Mobilization Web App** вЂ” a Next.js/TypeScript/Tailwind production app for the Ebikabyaffe Foundation, endorsed by the Buganda Council of Clan Heads (Olukiiko lw'Abataka). It helps Baganda discover their clan, verify their roots, give (Busuulu + donations), and connect with Bataka clan heads.

- **Key proverb**: "Agali awamu" вЂ” Enkulaakulana mu mbeera ne mu byenfuna (Unity is strength)

## Next task: `/roots` page

The Roots section is a sequential verification funnel. Build in this order:

### Files to create
1. `web/app/roots/page.tsx` вЂ” auth gate: show `<RootsGate />` if logged out, `<RootsContent />` if logged in
2. `web/components/roots/RootsGate.tsx` вЂ” "Sign in to trace your roots" prompt
3. `web/components/roots/RootsContent.tsx` вЂ” container with clan card, progress bar (5 steps), step cards
4. `web/components/roots/RootsSteps.tsx` вЂ” the 4 sequential step components (Siga, Mutuba, Lineage form, Verification request)

### Step flow (matches prototype-v2.html)
```
Step 0: Siga selection
  Options: Nkima, Mmamba, Ngabi, "I don't know my siga"
  в†’ unlocks Step 1

Step 1: Mutuba (subclan) selection
  Dynamic based on selected Siga
  в†’ unlocks Step 2

Step 2: Lineage form
  Fields: Full name, Father's name, Grandfather's name, Ssaza of origin
  в†’ unlocks Step 3

Step 3: Bataka verification request
  Shows submitted info, "Request Verification from Omutaka" CTA button
  в†’ shows "Verification pending" state
```
