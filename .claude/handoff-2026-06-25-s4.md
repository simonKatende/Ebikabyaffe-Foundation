### 1. `/give` page — Built from scratch (4 new files)

**`web/app/give/page.tsx`** — Thin entry file, renders `GiveContent`. No auth gate (give page is public).

**`web/components/give/GiveContent.tsx`** — Main container. Defines `Campaign` type and `CAMPAIGNS` array (one campaign: Wakivule School). **`web/components/give/OneTimeForm.tsx`** — One-time donation form.
- Currency toggle (UGX / USD)
- 4 preset amount buttons per currency (UGX: 5K/10K/25K/50K; USD: 2/5/10/20)
- Selecting a preset clears the custom amount field and vice versa
- **Phone number input appears immediately when MTN MoMo or Airtel is selected** (user's confirmed requirement)
- Submit disabled until: amount > 0 + payment method + phone (if mobile money)
- Success screen with "Make this a monthly Busuulu →" CTA
- Payment placeholders: Flutterwave (UGX), Stripe (USD/GBP)

**`web/components/give/BusuuluTiers.tsx`** — Monthly recurring tiers.
- 4 tiers: Omwana w'Ekika (UGX 5K/mo), Omugabo (UGX 20K/mo), Mukulu w'Ekika (UGX 50K/mo), Olukiiko lw'Ebikabyaffe (UGX 200K/mo — permanent gold border highlight)
- Selecting a tier expands its benefits panel
- **MTN MoMo phone input appears below cards once tier is selected** (user's confirmed requirement)
- **Continue button label shows chosen tier name + monthly amount** (user's confirmed requirement)
- Success screen confirms tier name and phone number
